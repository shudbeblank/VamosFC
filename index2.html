DETAILS

Vamos FC is a team consists of Putra Mahkota and Bangi boys mainly but we welcomed any players that would love to join us with open heart! Its not just a typical football club, its a family. Vamos FC started in the late 2020 during the pandemic. A group of friends, based in Bangi were having our daily “lepak” session at Warung Pak Teh Mak Teh (PTMT). We then decided to form a football club out of love and passion for the game. As of today, we have players from other cities as well. We are a team that is based around Klang Valley. All in all, its good to have you guys on board so we could share our experiences together. To winning games and be more united as a family! All hail Vamos!

p/s: most of the time around Bangi, Kajang and Cyberjaya.
